Minecraft Bedwars is a multiplayer game mode where players compete in teams to destroy the opposing team's bed while defending their own. The game takes place on an island with resources scattered around, which players can gather to purchase better equipment and weapons. Once a team's bed is destroyed, they can no longer respawn, and the last team standing wins the game.
